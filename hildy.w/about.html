About Me

Hello! I'm Hildy Wong, a designer that specialises in turning intricate data into captivating visual stories. I have an interactive media design background and am an expert in data visualisation and motion graphics, creating ideas that motivate action in addition to explaining insights.


I am aware of the difficulties faced by corporate e-commerce teams, HR specialists, and local marketing firms in today's data-driven world. I'm passionate about assisting you in achieving your goals of captivating your audience and effectively communicating ideas. My objective is to transform deluges of information into striking, lucid images that inspire and influence choices.

Every project presents an opportunity to make a lasting effect. Presentations can be improved, in my opinion, by using well-designed visualisations that make your data not only comprehensible but also captivating. I'm here to work with you to make your message pop out and give your data life.


Together, we can unlock your data's full potential. Together, we can produce images that enthral viewers and strengthen your brand!
